Setup Guide
Download · Install · Trial · Upgrade

Windows will flag the installer as unrecognised. This is normal for new software. These steps get you past it.

⚠ Why does this happen? StudyOps is new and hasn't been registered with Microsoft SmartScreen yet. The file is safe. The steps below tell you how to bypass the warning.
01
Click Download
Click the Download for Windows button. A warning appears in your browser's download bar at the bottom of the screen — don't dismiss it yet.
02
Open the three dots menu
Hover over the warning. Three dots ··· appear on the right. Click them to open the menu.
03
Select "Keep"
Choose Keep from the dropdown. A popup opens with more options.
04
Click the small arrow next to Delete
At the bottom of the popup, find the Delete button. To its right is a small downward arrow. Click that arrow — not the Delete button.
05
Click "Keep anyway"
Choose Keep anyway. The file finishes downloading to your machine.
Requires Windows 10 or 11 · 64-bit

Once the file is downloaded, run the installer and follow these five steps.

01
Open the installer
Open your Downloads folder and double-click StudyOps-Setup-v1.0.0.exe. The setup wizard launches.
02
Click Next
The welcome screen appears. Click Next to continue.
03
Check "Desktop shortcut"
Tick Create a desktop shortcut so you can launch StudyOps with one click. Then click Next.
04
Choose install location
Leave the default folder or click Browse to pick a different one. Click Next.
05
Click Install
Press Install. After it completes, the StudyOps shortcut appears on your desktop. Open it and you're in.
